Email used:
info@binxindustries.com.au

Before publishing:
1. Open contact/index.html
2. Replace "ADD YOUR PHONE NUMBER" with your real phone number.
3. Replace or add real photos in assets/ later as you get job photos.
4. Upload the folder to Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, GitHub Pages, or another static host.
5. Connect the domain:
   binxindustries.com.au

Notes:
- This is a static site, so it does not need paid GoDaddy hosting.
- The contact form opens the visitor's email app. It does not store messages on a server.
- To make a proper web form later, use a form service such as Netlify Forms, Formspree, Tally, or a custom backend.
