QUOTE CHECKLIST

What to send for a good quote.

The more clear the info is, the faster BINX can work out the right way to build it.

For fabrication

  • Photos of the area or broken part
  • Basic dimensions
  • Material preference if known
  • Load/strength requirements
  • Paint, finish or raw steel preference

For CNC cutting

  • DXF/SVG/PDF/sketch if you have one
  • Material and thickness
  • Quantity
  • Exact sizes or scale reference
  • Whether holes need to be accurate

For automation

  • What needs to turn on/off or be measured
  • Power source available
  • Distance/range needed
  • Indoor/outdoor environment
  • What should happen when something goes wrong
